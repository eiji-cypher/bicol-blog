About the Region

Land of Fire,
Coconut & Faith

Bicol occupies the long southeastern peninsula of Luzon, stretching toward the Visayas like a finger pointing to the rest of the archipelago. It is a land defined by contradictions — volcanoes and sea, burning chili and creamy gata, thundering devotion and quiet village life.

The region is home to six provinces, each with its own character, but unified by a shared love of coconut milk, a reverence for the Ina of Peñafrancia, and the ever-present silhouette of Mayon on the horizon.
